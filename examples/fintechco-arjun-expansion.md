# Example: FintechCo Southeast Asia Expansion

## Input Details

**Prospect:**
- Name: Arjun
- Title: Head of Sales (owns outbound and SDR performance)
- Company: FintechCo

**Account Signals:**
- Hiring 6 SDRs in the last 45 days
- Recently expanded into Southeast Asia

**Product/Service:**
- Helps sales teams handle complex product questions during live conversations without relying on sales engineers

**Context:**
- With a rapidly scaling SDR team and new market expansion, maintaining consistent messaging and reducing ramp time is likely becoming a bottleneck

---

## Draft v1 (Initial)

**Subject:** Onboarding 6 SDRs into Southeast Asia

**Body:**

Arjun,

Saw you've hired 6 SDRs in the last 45 days as you're pushing into Southeast Asia.

That kind of velocity usually creates a messaging consistency problem—especially when your reps hit technical questions they can't answer without looping in an SE.

We help sales teams surface the right answer to complex product questions during live calls, so SDRs don't stall or pass deals up the chain prematurely.

Reps get faster at handling objections, and your SEs stop getting pulled into early-stage calls that shouldn't need them yet.

Worth a 15-min look?

---

## Reviewer Feedback

**Verdict:** REVISE

**Priority Fixes:**

1. **Add attributable proof** - Missing specific metric or named customer validation
   - Suggested: "Deel's SDR team cut SE involvement in discovery calls by 40% in their first quarter"

2. **Split multi-idea sentence** - One sentence packs 3 concepts, making it hard to parse
   - Break into: (A) What you do, (B) The outcome

3. **Make capability concrete** - "Surface the right answer" is too vague
   - Suggested: "We surface technical answers from your product docs and past calls in real time while your SDRs are on the phone"

---
